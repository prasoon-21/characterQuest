The Writer

Alan Wake is a bestselling thriller novelist suffering from a two-year writer's block. He travels to the small town of Bright Falls, Washington with his wife Alice, hoping to find inspiration. Instead, he finds himself trapped in a nightmare where the darkness itself comes alive, and the manuscript pages he doesn't remember writing are coming true.

The Dark Presence

An ancient evil that dwells in Cauldron Lake. It can possess people, turning them into violent shells called the Taken.

It wants Alan to write it free.

The darkness fears only one thing: light.

It has waited centuries for the right artist.
