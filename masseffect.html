Profile

Commander Shepard is the first human Spectre and the galaxy's last hope against the Reapers—an ancient race of machines bent on exterminating all organic life. A soldier, leader, and legend, Shepard unites the galaxy's races against impossible odds.

Rank Commander / N7
Affiliation Systems Alliance
Status SPECTRE
Ship SSV Normandy

N7 Special Forces

Shepard earned the elite N7 designation—the highest level of proficiency in the Alliance military's special forces program. N7 soldiers are trained in advanced combat tactics, hostile environment survival, and leadership under fire.

First Human Spectre

Special Tactics and Reconnaissance agents answer only to the Citadel Council with authority to operate above the law.

The Normandy

A cutting-edge stealth frigate representing the best of human-turian engineering. Shepard's ship and home.

Paragon Path

Inspires through compassion, diplomacy, and unwavering moral principles. Saves lives at any cost.

Renegade Path

Gets results through intimidation, ruthlessness, and whatever means necessary. The mission comes first.

Biotic Powers

Manipulate dark energy to lift enemies, create barriers, and unleash devastating singularities.

The Reaper Threat

Ancient machine gods that harvest all advanced organic life every 50,000 years. They are patient, relentless, and seemingly invincible. Shepard is the only one who believes the threat is real—until it's almost too late.

Garrus Vakarian

Turian sharpshooter and Shepard's most loyal friend. "There's no Shepard without Vakarian."

Tali'Zorah

Quarian engineer with unmatched technical skills. A trusted companion from the very beginning.

Liara T'Soni

Asari scientist, Prothean expert, and the Shadow Broker. Her knowledge is invaluable to the mission.

The Suicide Mission

Shepard leads a team through the Omega-4 Relay on a mission no one is expected to survive.

Every choice, every loyalty earned, determines who lives and who dies.

The Collectors must be stopped. The cost will be high.

Virmire

An impossible choice—only one squad member can survive. This moment defines the cost of command.

Death & Rebirth

Shepard dies saving the crew. Cerberus spends two years on the Lazarus Project to bring them back.

Uniting the Galaxy

With the Reapers invading, Shepard must forge alliances between ancient enemies—Krogan and Turian, Quarian and Geth. The galaxy's survival depends on unity. Old grudges must be set aside, or everyone dies.

Earth Burns

The Reapers reach Earth. Millions die. Shepard must leave to build the forces needed to take it back.

The Crucible

An ancient superweapon designed by countless civilizations. Humanity's only hope against extinction.

The Catalyst

Destroy. Control. Synthesize. Three choices. No perfect endings. Only Shepard can decide.
